Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information about website visitors through our anonymous visitor identification technology, including names, email addresses, phone numbers, and browsing behavior data.

2. How We Use Information

Information collected is used solely for lead generation purposes and is delivered directly to our clients' CRM systems. We do not sell or share visitor data with third parties.

3. Data Security

Security is our top priority. We implement enterprise-grade security measures to protect all collected data:

  • All data transmission uses bank-level encryption (TLS 1.3+)
  • Data storage is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • CRM credentials are encrypted and never exposed
  • We maintain SOC 2 compliance and conduct regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication protect all systems
  • We follow industry best practices for data protection

4. Your Rights

You have the right to opt out of data collection at any time. Contact us to request data deletion or opt-out.

5. Compliance

We comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable privacy regulations. All data collection uses publicly available information and verified databases.

6. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at: privacy@flowclose.com
